SWITCHES

Customize even the sound and touch

The switch is a type of switch found inside mechanical keyboards, it is capsule-shaped and mobile. Its function is to give precision in the keystrokes of each key, either by activating them in the middle of the journey or at the end of it. They work with a spring that resides in each of the keys, this spring is accompanied by a trigger and they are responsible for the sensation that the keyboard produces when you use it (more or less noise, resistance to pressing...)
